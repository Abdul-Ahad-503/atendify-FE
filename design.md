# AttendX Design System

## 1. Color Scheme (Modern & Professional)

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Color (Blue)**: `#2563EB`
- **Primary Hover (Dark Blue)**: `#1E40AF`
- **Secondary Color (Teal)**: `#14B8A6`

### Background & Surface
- **Background (Light Gray)**: `#F8FAFC`
- **Surface / Card (White)**: `#FFFFFF`

### Text Colors
- **Text – Primary (Dark Gray)**: `#0F172A`
- **Text – Secondary (Gray)**: `#475569`

### Borders & Dividers
- **Border / Divider (Light Border)**: `#E2E8F0`

### Status Colors
- **Success (Green)**: `#22C55E`
- **Warning (Yellow)**: `#FACC15`
- **Error (Red)**: `#EF4444`

---

## 2. Typography (Free Google Fonts)

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Inter
- **Fallback**: system-ui, sans-serif

### Font Sizes
| Type | Size | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Heading 1 (Page Title) | 32px | SemiBold |
| Heading 2 (Section Title) | 24px | SemiBold |
| Heading 3 | 20px | Medium |
| Body Text | 16px | Regular |
| Small Text / Labels | 14px | Regular |
| Extra Small (Hints) | 12px | Regular |

### Line Height
- **Headings**: 120%
- **Body**: 150%

---

## 3. Spacing System (8px Grid)

- **4px** – Extra small spacing
- **8px** – Small
- **16px** – Normal
- **24px** – Section spacing
- **32px** – Large
- **48px** – Page padding

> Use Auto Layout in Figma with 8px increments.
